Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation Time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with salt and pepper until they are well mixed. Optionally, add a tablespoon of water or milk.
  2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Cook the omelette: Pour the eggs into the pan and tilt it to spread the eggs evenly.
  4. Add fillings (optional): Sprinkle fillings when the omelette starts to set but is still runny.
  5. Fold and serve: Fold the omelette over the fillings and cook for another minute.
  6. Enjoy: Serve hot with extra salt and pepper if needed.
